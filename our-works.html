Tipene Funerals

Tipene Funerals are a funeral home based in Auckland. They offer full funeral services and have gained a household name thanks to the TVNZ and Netflix show – The Casketeers.

Francis and Kaiora Tipene run Tipene Funerals and share a passion for providing professional and compassionate services to everyone during their time of need.

The Challenge:

Troubled by an existing that was, slow, dated and lacking in professional design, Ryze was approached by Kaiora to help her redesign and redevelop the company website. We were tasked with redesigning the website and building it from the ground up, on a stable, reliable and modern platform. After the website was live, we installed Google Analytics and started monitoring traffic and activity on the site.
